Audience

Persona 1

Picture

Demographics

"Xavier" is a 30-year-old man who has recently started his own business.

Motivation

Xavier is motivated by a need to grow his new business to protect his interests.

Xavier is looking for ways to engage with other business owners in the area, to get mentorship and advertizing opportunities.

Xavier wants to be able to interact and network with those in the area to continuously build his business.

Xavier needs an easy way to connect with the local chamber of commerce.

Persona 2

Picture

Demographics

"Chantel" is a 35-year-old investor.

Motivation

Chantel is motivated to grow her companies that she has invested in in hopes that she can make money to provide for her family back in the Bahamas.

Chantel wants to find stable, local companies who want to grow so she can invest in them. She knows the area is full of good opportunities, but she doesn't know where to start looking.

Chantel wants to look at the list of businesses who have already joined the chamber of commerce so she can do some research. She also wants to engage with the board of directors about possibly advertising her services.

Chantel needs to find local companies and engage with local leadership of the chamber.
